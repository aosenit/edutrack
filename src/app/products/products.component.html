Product Features

Features For Parents

SchoolTrack gives parents secured accesses to their children’s progress from home to enable them proactively participate in the children’s educational development. Parents can:

Access homework & lecture notes

View grades, progress reports & attendance reports

View invoice and payment schedule

Online tuition and fee payment

View transcripts

View discipline & health records

View class announcements

Access school events calendar

Communicate with teachers

Features For Students

On SchoolTrack Students will have secured access to online learning resources provided by their teachers and school administrators to ensure their continuous engagement both within and outside the walls of the school as they:

Access and submit completed homework

View class announcements

Communicate with their teachers

Access class lecture notes and quizzes

Access school events calendar

Access online learning resources

Features For Teachers

SchoolTrack offers teachers immeasurable empowerments to not only efficiently deliver educational contents but also to enjoy doing so effortlessly. SchoolTrack confers endless possibilities on teacher:

Online Assignment Calendar

Easy upload of assignments, schedules, files, and class events for 24/7 access by students and their parents

Instant generation of student progress reports, and easy printing of paper reports

Secure, spam-free and seamless online messaging with colleagues, students and their parents

Anywhere, anytime access to school information and class preparation

Easy customisation of gradebook to exact school and course grading system

Instant generation of student progress reports, and easy printing of paper reports

Easy tracking of student attendance and seamless aggregation of same into required school administrative reports
